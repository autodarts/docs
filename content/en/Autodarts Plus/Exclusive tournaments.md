Exclusive & Private Tournaments
We’re leveling up the competitive scene!

Exclusive Tournaments: Reserved for dedicated players, featuring AI Referee support and serious competition.
Private Tournaments: Host your own arena! Full control to curate the roster, set custom formats, and dictate the rules for close-knit rivalry with your chosen circle.

Exclusive Tournaments

You can recognize exclusive tournaments by the lightning bolt icon, or you can enable the ‘Plus Only’ filter

![Exclusive Tournament](/plus/images/exclusivetournament.png)

Private Tournaments

{{<hint type=note icon=gdoc_info_outline >}}
There is a limit of 8 players for both online and local tournaments. For online tournament every player needs to have it's own board.
{{< /hint >}}

To create your own private tournament, click the plus icon in the top-right corner of the tournament page.

![Private Tournament](/plus/images/privatetournament.png)

![Private Tournament create](/plus/images/createtournament.png)

You can invite friends directly from your friends list, via an invitation link, or using a QR code.

![invite](/plus/images/invite.png)
