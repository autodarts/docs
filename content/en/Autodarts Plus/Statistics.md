Advanced Statistics: Serious Data for Serious Players,
Get the data you need to grow! This feature provides a detailed personal dashboard to track your performance over time.

Track Your Progress: View match volume, accuracy, and scoring trends.,
Real Insight: Spot weaknesses and celebrate improvements across selectable timeframes (days, weeks, months).

{{<hint type=note icon=gdoc_info_outline >}}
Statistics are currently only available for X01.
{{< /hint >}}

You can access the advanced statistics by clicking the X01+ tab on your Statistics page

![Statistics TAB](/plus/images/statistics_tab.png)

You can also sort your statistics by time using the built-in filter functionality

![Statistics Filter](/plus/images/statistics_filter.png)
